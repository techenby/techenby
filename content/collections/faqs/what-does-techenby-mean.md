TechEnby is a combination of Technology and Enby.

Enby is a phonetic spelling of the abbreviation NB, a term used to describe non-binary individuals (like me!) - those who don't identify exclusively as male or female. [Source](https://queer-word.beehiiv.com/p/what-does-enby-mean)
