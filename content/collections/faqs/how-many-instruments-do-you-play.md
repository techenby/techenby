6: Clarinet, Trumpet, [Mellophone](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mellophone), Alto & Tenor Sax, and Guitar.

The clarinet was my first instrument that I started in 5th grade, 23 years ago. Then I learned the saxophone in 8th grade for Jazz band. In High School I taught myself how to play the Guitar. Finally, in college I learned the Trumpet and Mellophone.

To this day I still play the Clarinet and Trumpet regularly in the [Lakeside Pride](https://lakesidepride.org) Symphonic and Pops ensembles. Band and music has been a fundamental part of my life for as long as I can remember, and I highly recommend putting your children in music and picking up your old instruments for fun.
